**Total Micro-Learning Cards**: ~100 individual concept cards

**Note**: Each micro-learning card:
- Takes 5 minutes or less to complete
- Includes visual aids (diagrams, videos)
- Has a knowledge check (2-3 questions)
- Unlocks related action cards in the deck
- Uses plain language (6th-8th grade level)
- Provides "deeper dive" links for curious learners

---
