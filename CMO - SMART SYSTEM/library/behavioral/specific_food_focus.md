# SPECIFIC FOOD FOCUS

**Tier 1**: Behavioral Cards  
**Tier 2**: Nutrition  
**Tier 3**: Specific Food Focus

**Estimated Cards in Category**: ~40-50

---

## TIER 4: INCREASE SPECIFIC FOODS (~25 cards)

### Vegetables & Fruits
- Eat 5 Servings of Vegetables Daily
- Eat 2 Servings of Fruit Daily
- Increase Leafy Greens (2+ servings/day)
- Eat Cruciferous Vegetables 3x/week (Broccoli, Cauliflower)
- Increase Colorful Vegetables (Rainbow Eating)
- Add Berries to Daily Diet

### Proteins
- Eat Fish 2x/week (Especially Fatty Fish for Omega-3)
- Include Plant-Based Protein Daily
- Eat Lean Poultry 3-4x/week
- Add Legumes/Beans 3-4x/week

### Healthy Fats
- Consume Nuts Daily (1 oz or small handful)
- Use Olive Oil as Primary Fat
- Include Avocado 3-4x/week
- Eat Seeds Daily (Chia, Flax, Pumpkin)

### Whole Grains
- Choose Whole Grains Over Refined
- Eat Oats/Oatmeal for Breakfast
- Include Quinoa or Brown Rice

### Other Beneficial Foods
- Eat Probiotic Foods Daily (Yogurt, Kefir)
- Include Fermented Foods (Kimchi, Sauerkraut)
- Drink Green Tea Daily
- Consume Dark Chocolate (70%+ cacao) in moderation
- Add Herbs and Spices (Turmeric, Ginger, Garlic)
- Increase Water Intake (8 glasses/day)

---

## TIER 4: REDUCE/LIMIT SPECIFIC FOODS (~15 cards)

### Sodium
- Reduce Sodium to <2300mg/day
- Reduce Sodium to <1500mg/day
- Limit Processed/Packaged Foods
- Avoid Adding Salt at Table

### Added Sugars
- Limit Added Sugars to <25g/day
- Eliminate Sugary Beverages
- Reduce Desserts to 1-2x/week
- Choose No-Sugar-Added Options

### Unhealthy Fats
- Limit Saturated Fat to <10% of Calories
- Eliminate Trans Fats
- Reduce Fried Foods

### Processed Foods
- Limit Ultra-Processed Foods
- Reduce Red Meat to <2 servings/week
- Limit Processed Meats (Deli Meat, Bacon)

### Alcohol
- Limit Alcohol (Men: ≤2 drinks/day; Women: ≤1 drink/day)
- Abstain from Alcohol

---

## TIER 4: FOOD SUBSTITUTIONS (~5 cards)

- Swap Refined Grains for Whole Grains
- Replace Butter with Olive Oil
- Substitute Greek Yogurt for Sour Cream/Mayo
- Use Herbs/Spices Instead of Salt
- Choose Water or Unsweetened Beverages Over Sugary Drinks
