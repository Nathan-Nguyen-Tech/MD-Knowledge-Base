## PILLAR DEFINITION

**Emotion Pillar** covers mental health, stress management, mood regulation, cognitive techniques, and psychological wellbeing.

**Rule**: An Emotion card answers: "What ONE specific mental/psychological technique should I do right now?"

**Placement Logic**:
- If it targets MENTAL/PSYCHOLOGICAL state (mood, thoughts, emotions) â†’ Emotion
- If it targets PHYSICAL restoration (sleep, body) â†’ Recovery
- If it involves EXTERNAL factors or other people â†’ Environment

## Card Template Reminder

Each card uses the **UNIVERSAL CARD TEMPLATE**:
- **WHO**: Patient practices; Therapist/Counselor teaches
- **WHAT**: The specific technique (from this library)
- **HOW**: Step-by-step instructions
- **WHEN**: Frequency, optimal timing (CUSTOMIZABLE)
- **WHERE**: Anywhere quiet, home, work
- **MEASURE**: Mood rating (1-10), anxiety rating, practice log
- **TARGET**: Emotional goals (CUSTOMIZABLE)
- **SAFETY**: When to seek help, crisis resources, escalation criteria
- **FOLLOW-UP**: Technique review, adjustment
