## Card Naming Convention

**Format**: `NUT_[CATEGORY]_[NUMBER]`

**Category Abbreviations**:
- `DIET` = Evidence-Based Diet Patterns
- `THER` = Therapeutic Diet Patterns
- `MACRO` = Macronutrient-Focused
- `ADD` = Increase Foods
- `RED` = Reduce Foods
- `SWAP` = Food Substitutions
- `BEH` = Eating Behaviors
- `TIME` = Meal Timing
- `PLAN` = Meal Planning

## Notes

1. **Individualization**: All nutrition cards should note need for personalization based on preferences/culture
2. **Medical supervision**: Therapeutic diets should involve dietitian/provider guidance
3. **Contraindications**: Include when patterns may not be appropriate (e.g., keto in pancreatitis)
4. **Cultural considerations**: Adapt food examples for diverse populations
5. **Cost considerations**: Include budget-friendly options

---

## Clinical Pearls for Card Development

1. **Mediterranean Cards**: Include food pyramid visual, specific food examples, olive oil emphasis
2. **Low-Sodium Cards**: Include hidden sodium sources, label reading, fresh vs processed
3. **Diabetic Cards**: Include carb counting basics, glycemic index, meal balance
4. **IF Cards**: Include who should NOT do IF (pregnancy, eating disorders, diabetes on insulin)
5. **Portion Control Cards**: Include hand-size guides, plate method visual
