- `SOC` = Social Connection
- `AIR` = Indoor Air Quality
- `HOME` = Home Safety
- `EXP` = Environmental Exposures
- `TOB` = Tobacco Cessation
- `ALC` = Alcohol Moderation
- `WORK` = Occupational Health
- `FIN` = Financial Health
- `LIGHT` = Light Exposure & Circadian
- `NATURE` = Grounding & Nature Exposure
- `MICRO` = Workplace Micro-Actions

## WHAT IS NOT A CARD (These are DECKS)

The following are **DECKS** (collections of cards), NOT individual cards:
- ❌ "Smoking Cessation Program" → DECK containing multiple tobacco cards
- ❌ "Fall Prevention Program" → DECK combining home safety + exercise cards
- ❌ "Social Wellness Plan" → DECK of social connection cards
- ❌ "Workplace Wellness Program" → DECK of occupational cards

---

## HOW DECKS ARE ASSEMBLED

**Example: Smoking Cessation Deck**
Composed of individual cards:
1. ENV_TOB_001: Set Quit Date
2. ENV_TOB_002: Remove Cigarettes from Home/Car
3. ENV_TOB_003: Identify Smoking Triggers
4. ENV_TOB_004: Use Nicotine Replacement (Patch)
5. ENV_TOB_007: Call Quitline
6. ENV_TOB_010: Tell Others You're Quitting
7. REC_STRESS_007: Write in Journal (from Recovery library)
8. REC_BREATH_001: Diaphragmatic Breathing (from Recovery library)

**Example: Home Fall Prevention Deck**
1. ENV_HOME_001: Remove Tripping Hazards
2. ENV_HOME_002: Install Grab Bars
3. ENV_HOME_003: Improve Lighting
4. ENV_HOME_004: Secure Rugs
5. MOV_BAL_001: Single-Leg Stand (from Movement library)

## Card Template Reminder

Each card uses the **UNIVERSAL CARD TEMPLATE**:
- **WHO**: Patient implements; Social worker/counselor guides
- **WHAT**: The specific action (from this library)
- **HOW**: Step-by-step implementation
- **WHEN**: Frequency, timeline (CUSTOMIZABLE)
- **WHERE**: Home, work, community
- **MEASURE**: Specific metrics (drinks/week, smoke-free days, etc.)
- **TARGET**: Defined goals (CUSTOMIZABLE)
- **SAFETY**: Crisis resources, escalation criteria
- **FOLLOW-UP**: Check-in schedule, progress review
