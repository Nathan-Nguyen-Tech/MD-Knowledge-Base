---

## CRITICAL CHANGES IN v2.0

**What Changed:**
1. **Removed all "routine/program" items** - Those are DECKS, not cards
2. **Removed duration/intensity variants** - Template handles customization
3. **Each card = ONE specific atomic movement/exercise**
4. **Patient can do this action independently**

**Rule**: If you can't describe it in one verb + one object, it's not a card.

---

## Overview

This document enumerates ALL individual Tier 5 cards for Movement and Exercise interventions. Each entry represents ONE SMART card - a single, specific, atomic action that patients can perform.

**Total Cards**: 378 cards

**Action Types Included**:
- Treatment/Intervention (exercises, poses, movements)
- Monitoring (tracking metrics)
- Assessment (fitness testing)
- Preparation (pre-exercise setup)
- Maintenance (equipment care, recovery)

## WHAT IS NOT IN THE CARD NAME (Template handles these)

The following are **TEMPLATE PARAMETERS**, not card variations:
- ‚ùå Duration (10 min, 20 min, 30 min)
- ‚ùå Intensity (light, moderate, vigorous)
- ‚ùå Frequency (3x/week, daily)
- ‚ùå Sets and reps (3x10, 4x12)
- ‚ùå RPE level (RPE 5, RPE 7)
- ‚ùå Heart rate zone (Zone 2, Zone 3)

**Example**: There is ONE "Walk" card. The template customizes it to "Walk for 20 minutes at moderate pace (RPE 5-6) 5 days per week."

## Card Template Reminder

Each card uses the **UNIVERSAL CARD TEMPLATE**:
- **WHO**: Patient performs; PT/Coach designs
- **WHAT**: The specific exercise (from this library)
- **HOW**: Technique cues, form guidance, modifications
- **WHEN**: Frequency, duration (CUSTOMIZABLE)
- **WHERE**: Home, gym, outdoors
- **MEASURE**: HR, reps, sets, time (CUSTOMIZABLE)
- **TARGET**: Performance goals (CUSTOMIZABLE)
- **SAFETY**: Exercise-specific precautions
- **FOLLOW-UP**: Progress review schedule
