# AEROBIC EXERCISE

**Tier 1**: Behavioral Cards  
**Tier 2**: Movement  
**Tier 3**: Aerobic Exercise

**Estimated Cards in Category**: ~35-40

---

## TIER 4: WALKING (~10 cards)

### Beginner Walking Programs
- Walk 10 Minutes, 3x/Week (RPE 3-4)
- Walk 15 Minutes, 3x/Week (RPE 4-5)
- Walk 20 Minutes, 4x/Week (RPE 5-6)

### Intermediate Walking Programs
- Walk 30 Minutes, 5x/Week (RPE 5-6)
- Walk 30 Minutes, 5x/Week with Intervals
- Walk 45 Minutes, 4x/Week (RPE 6-7)

### Advanced Walking Programs
- Walk 60 Minutes, 5x/Week (RPE 6-7)
- Power Walking 30 Minutes, 5x/Week (RPE 7-8)

### Specialty Walking
- Walk 10,000 Steps Daily (Tracked)
- Nature Walking/Hiking (Varied Terrain)

---

## TIER 4: RUNNING/JOGGING (~8 cards)

### Beginner Running (Couch to 5K Style)
- Walk/Jog Intervals 20 Minutes, 3x/Week
- Jog 15 Minutes Continuously, 3x/Week
- Jog 20 Minutes, 3x/Week (Build to 30 min)

### Intermediate Running
- Run 30 Minutes, 3x/Week (RPE 6-7)
- Run 30 Minutes, 4x/Week
- Run 5K (3.1 miles), 3x/Week

### Advanced Running
- Run 45-60 Minutes, 4-5x/Week
- Interval Training (HIIT) 20-30 Minutes

---

## TIER 4: CYCLING (~6 cards)

### Stationary/Outdoor Cycling
- Cycle 20 Minutes, 3x/Week (Moderate pace)
- Cycle 30 Minutes, 4x/Week (Moderate pace)
- Cycle 45 Minutes, 3x/Week
- Cycle 60 Minutes, 3x/Week
- Interval Cycling (High-Low Intensity) 30 Minutes
- Spin Class 2-3x/Week

## TIER 4: SWIMMING (~5 cards)

- Swim 20 Minutes, 2-3x/Week (Continuous)
- Swim 30 Minutes, 3x/Week (Laps)
- Swim 45 Minutes, 3x/Week
- Aqua Aerobics Class 2x/Week
- Water Walking/Jogging 20-30 Minutes

## TIER 4: ELLIPTICAL/ROWING (~4 cards)

- Elliptical 20 Minutes, 3x/Week
- Elliptical 30 Minutes, 4x/Week
- Rowing Machine 20 Minutes, 3x/Week
- Rowing Machine 30 Minutes, 3x/Week

## TIER 4: GROUP FITNESS CLASSES (~5 cards)

- Aerobics Class 2-3x/Week
- Zumba/Dance Fitness 2x/Week
- Step Aerobics 2x/Week
- Kickboxing Cardio 2x/Week
- HIIT Class 2x/Week

**Total Tier 5 Cards**: ~38 individual aerobic exercise cards

**Note**: Each card includes:
- Intensity guidance (RPE, heart rate zones, METs)
- Duration and frequency
- Progression tiers (how to advance)
- Equipment needed
- Safety modifications (joint issues, cardiac conditions)
- Warm-up and cool-down
- Tracking methods (time, distance, HR)
