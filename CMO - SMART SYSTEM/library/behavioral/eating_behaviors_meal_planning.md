## TIER 4: EATING BEHAVIORS (~20 cards)

### Mindful Eating Practices
- Practice Mindful Eating (Slow Down, Savor)
- Eat Without Distractions (No TV/Phone)
- Chew Food Thoroughly (20-30 chews per bite)
- Use Smaller Plates and Bowls
- Pause Halfway Through Meals

### Portion Control
- Measure Portions for 2 Weeks (Learning Phase)
- Use Hand-Sizing Method for Portions
- Follow the Plate Method (1/2 Vegetables, 1/4 Protein, 1/4 Grains)
- Stop Eating at 80% Full (Hara Hachi Bu)
- Serve from Kitchen, Not Table (Reduce Seconds)

### Eating Timing & Frequency
- Eat 3 Balanced Meals Daily
- Include 1-2 Healthy Snacks If Needed
- Don't Skip Breakfast
- Avoid Late-Night Eating (No Food 2-3 Hours Before Bed)
- Consistent Meal Times Daily

### Intermittent Fasting Patterns
- 16:8 Intermittent Fasting (16-hour fast, 8-hour eating window)
- 12:12 Intermittent Fasting (12-hour fast)
- 5:2 Intermittent Fasting (5 days normal, 2 days reduced calories)

### Appetite & Hunger Management
- Distinguish Hunger from Cravings (HALT Check)
- Drink Water Before Meals
- Eat Protein with Every Meal (Increases Satiety)
- Include Fiber-Rich Foods (Increases Fullness)

## TIER 4: MEAL PLANNING & PREPARATION (~15 cards)

### Weekly Meal Planning
- Plan All Meals for the Week (Sunday Prep)
- Prep Lunches for the Week on Sunday
- Batch Cook Proteins on Weekends
- Pre-Portion Snacks in Containers
- Create Weekly Grocery List Before Shopping

### Daily Meal Structure
- Prepare Balanced Breakfast in Advance
- Pack Lunch (Don't Rely on Takeout)
- Plan Healthy Dinners 5 Nights/Week
- Keep Emergency Meals on Hand (Frozen, Pantry)

### Cooking Skills & Strategies
- Learn 5 Go-To Healthy Recipes
- Master Basic Cooking Techniques (Roasting, Grilling, Sautéing)
- Use Slow Cooker for Easy Meals
- Cook Extra Servings for Leftovers
- Organize Kitchen for Healthy Eating (Visible Fruits/Veggies)

### Dining Out Strategies
- Review Restaurant Menus in Advance
- Order Vegetables First
- Request Dressing/Sauce on the Side
- Split Entrées or Take Half Home
- Limit Dining Out to 1-2x/Week
