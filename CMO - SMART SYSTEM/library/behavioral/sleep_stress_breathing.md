## TIER 4: SLEEP HYGIENE & OPTIMIZATION (~25 cards)

### Sleep Schedule
- Maintain Consistent Sleep Schedule (Same Bedtime/Wake Time)
- Go to Bed by 10 PM (or Target Time)
- Wake at Same Time Daily (Even Weekends)
- Aim for 7-9 Hours Sleep Nightly
- Gradual Sleep Schedule Adjustment (15 min increments)

### Pre-Bed Routine
- Establish 30-Minute Wind-Down Routine
- Digital Sunset (No Screens 1 Hour Before Bed)
- Digital Sunset (No Screens 2 Hours Before Bed)
- Read Before Bed (Physical Book, 20-30 min)
- Take Warm Bath/Shower Before Bed

### Sleep Environment
- Keep Bedroom Cool (60-67Â°F)
- Make Bedroom Dark (Blackout Curtains, Eye Mask)
- Keep Bedroom Quiet (White Noise, Earplugs)
- Reserve Bed for Sleep Only (No Work, TV)
- Use Comfortable Mattress and Pillows

### Daytime Sleep Hygiene
- Get Morning Sunlight Exposure (15-30 min)
- Avoid Caffeine After 2 PM
- Limit Naps to 20-30 Minutes (Before 3 PM)
- Exercise Regularly (But Not Close to Bedtime)
- Limit Alcohol Intake (Especially Before Bed)

### Sleep Disorders Management
- CBT-I Techniques (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia)
- Stimulus Control Therapy
- Sleep Restriction Therapy
- Paradoxical Intention (For Performance Anxiety)

## TIER 4: STRESS MANAGEMENT TECHNIQUES (~20 cards)

### Breathing Exercises
- 4-7-8 Breathing (5 minutes, 2x/day)
- Box Breathing (4-4-4-4, 5 minutes)
- Diaphragmatic Breathing (Belly Breathing, 10 minutes)
- Alternate Nostril Breathing (Nadi Shodhana, 5 minutes)
- Physiological Sigh (Quick Stress Relief)

### Progressive Relaxation
- Progressive Muscle Relaxation (Full Body, 15 minutes)
- Progressive Muscle Relaxation (Quick Version, 5 minutes)
- Body Scan Meditation (10 minutes)

### Mindfulness & Meditation
- Mindfulness Meditation (10 minutes daily)
- Mindfulness Meditation (20 minutes daily)
- Loving-Kindness Meditation (Metta, 10 minutes)
- Walking Meditation (10-15 minutes)

### Cognitive Techniques
- Journaling for Stress Relief (10 minutes, evening)
- Worry Time (Scheduled 15-minute worry period)
- Gratitude Practice (List 3 Things Daily)
- Positive Self-Talk Exercises
- Cognitive Reframing (Challenge Negative Thoughts)

### Physical Stress Release
- Gentle Yoga for Stress (20 minutes)
- Nature Walks for Mental Health (30 minutes, 3x/week)
- Creative Activities (Art, Music, 30 min)
