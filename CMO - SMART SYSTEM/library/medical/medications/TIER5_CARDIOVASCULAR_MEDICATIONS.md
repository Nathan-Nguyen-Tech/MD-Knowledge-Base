## Card Naming Convention

**Format**: `MED_CARDIO_[SUBCLASS]_[NUMBER]`

**Examples**:
- `MED_CARDIO_ACE_001` = Lisinopril
- `MED_CARDIO_BB_003` = Carvedilol
- `MED_CARDIO_STATIN_001` = Atorvastatin

---

## Notes

1. **Titratable doses within single card**: Each card includes all dose tiers (e.g., Lisinopril card covers 2.5-40mg)
2. **Combination products**: Listed separately when clinically distinct (e.g., BiDil)
3. **Formulation differences**: Listed separately when clinically relevant (e.g., Metoprolol Succinate vs Tartrate)
4. **Not included**: IV-only hospital medications (except where outpatient relevant)
