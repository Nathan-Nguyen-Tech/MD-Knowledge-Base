## KEY DESIGN PRINCIPLES REFLECTED

1. **One Action Per Card**: Each Tier 5 card = one specific, measurable action
2. **Titratable Medications**: Single card per drug (not separate cards per dose)
3. **Atomic Actions**: "Walk 30 minutes" not "Walking Program" (programs = decks)
4. **File Organization**: Tier 3 files stop at logical clinical categories
5. **Progressive Complexity**: Beginner â†’ Intermediate â†’ Advanced within same category
6. **Evidence-Based**: All cards grounded in clinical guidelines and research
7. **Practical Focus**: Every card must be actionable and trackable
8. **Cross-Cutting Tags**: Flexible filtering beyond strict hierarchy
9. **Education Embedded**: Micro-learning cards provide brief foundational knowledge
10. **Deck Assembly**: Complex interventions (like "Sleep Hygiene") = multiple individual cards in a deck
