### CARD 3: ğŸ¥— DASH Diet

**Quick Summary**:
- Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension
- Expected effect: 8-14 mmHg reduction
- Focus: High fruits/vegetables, low sodium
- Time: 2-3 hours meal prep weekly

- 8-10 servings fruits/vegetables daily
- Whole grains instead of refined
- Lean proteins (fish, poultry)
- Low-fat dairy
- Limit sodium to <2,300mg (ideally <1,500mg)

**Synergy**: Amplifies Card 1 (medication) effects; natural fit with Card 5 (walking) for overall cardiovascular health

### CARD 4: ğŸƒ Walking Exercise Program

**Progression Tiers**:
- Tier 1 (Weeks 1-2): 10 min, 3x/week, RPE 3-4
- Tier 2 (Weeks 3-6): 20 min, 4x/week, RPE 5-6
- Tier 3 (Ongoing): 30 min, 5x/week, RPE 6-7

**Synergy**: Complements Card 3 (DASH diet) for weight management and cardiovascular fitness

### CARD 5: ğŸ”¬ Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP) Lab

- Blood test to monitor kidney function and potassium

- Why: Lisinopril can affect K+ and creatinine
- Fasting: Not required

**What's Being Checked**:
- Potassium (K+): Should stay 3.5-5.0 mEq/L
- Creatinine (Cr): Monitor kidney function
- Sodium (Na): Confirm dietary changes working

**Synergy**: Safety monitoring for Card 1 (medication); validates effectiveness of Card 3 (low sodium diet)

2. Hold breath for 7 counts

**Synergy**: Reduces acute stress responses that can spike BP; complements all other cards by improving overall stress management
