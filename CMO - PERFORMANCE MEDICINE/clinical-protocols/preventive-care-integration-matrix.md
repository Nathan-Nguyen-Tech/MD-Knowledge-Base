# Preventive Care Integration Matrix

## Overview

This document integrates **USPSTF Grade A and B recommendations** (evidence-based preventive services with high-certainty net benefit) into the **8 Health Systems Framework** of Performance Medicine.

**Purpose**: Ensure that preventive care is systematically integrated into every patient's care plan based on age, sex, and risk factors, mapped directly to the Health Matrix dashboard.

---

## USPSTF Grading System (Quick Reference)

- **Grade A**: High certainty of substantial net benefit. Clinicians should offer/provide this service.
- **Grade B**: High certainty of moderate net benefit, OR moderate certainty of moderate-to-substantial net benefit. Clinicians should offer/provide this service.

**This document includes only Grade A and B recommendations** - services proven to improve health outcomes.

## Clinical Workflow: Preventive Care Integration

### Step 1: Age and Risk Stratification
- Identify patient age bracket
- Identify risk factors (family history, comorbidities, lifestyle)

### Step 2: Generate Preventive Care Checklist
- Pull USPSTF A/B recommendations for age + sex + risk factors
- Map to 8 Health Systems

### Step 3: Check Completion Status
- Which screenings are due?
- Which are up to date?
- Which have abnormal results requiring follow-up?

### Step 4: Populate Health Matrix
- Assign preventive care results to appropriate system/domain
- Use traffic-light status (Green/Yellow/Orange/Red)

### Step 5: Create Unified Prescription
- Overdue screenings â†’ Schedule
- Abnormal results â†’ Intervention plan (medical + lifestyle)
- Upcoming screenings â†’ Patient education and preparation

## Quarterly Preventive Care Review

At each quarterly visit, review:

1. **Due screenings** - What's coming up in next 3-6 months?
2. **Overdue screenings** - What needs to be scheduled immediately?
3. **Abnormal results** - What needs intervention or follow-up?
4. **Vaccination status** - What's due based on age and risk?

**Goal**: No preventive care item falls through the cracks. Every A/B recommendation integrated into care plan.
