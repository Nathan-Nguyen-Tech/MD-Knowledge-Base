# Performance Medicine AI Agent Repository

## Project Overview

This repository contains the foundational knowledge architecture, domain expertise structure, and reference materials for an AI agent specializing in **Performance Medicine** - a unified medical specialty that integrates multiple disciplines to optimize human health and performance across all life domains.

## What is Performance Medicine?

Performance Medicine is a comprehensive, evidence-based medical specialty that synthesizes knowledge from 13+ integrated domains:

- Family Medicine
- Internal Medicine
- Sports Medicine
- Exercise Physiology and Kinesiology
- Functional Medicine
- Lifestyle Medicine
- Integrative and Complementary Medicine
- Nutrition Medicine
- Behavioral and Mental Health Medicine
- Athletic Medicine
- Military Medicine
- Executive Health and Optimization
- Longevity Medicine (evidence-based only)

### Core Clinical Philosophy

Unlike traditional medicine that primarily targets **disease control**, Performance Medicine targets three interconnected performance capabilities:

1. **Capacity** - What you can do today (functional performance, lived experience of health)
2. **Resilience** - How quickly you recover (bounce back from stress, illness, injury, exertion)
3. **Flexibility** - How well you adapt (metabolic, circadian, training, and stress adaptability)

These are **measurable, trackable clinical endpoints** that sit alongside traditional risk markers like blood pressure, glucose, and lipids.

### Target Populations

This specialty optimizes health and performance for:
- **Athletes** - competitive and recreational
- **Military personnel** - tactical athletes and operators
- **High-functioning executives** - cognitive and physical optimization
- **General population** - preventive and performance-focused care

## The AI Agent's Role

The Performance Medicine AI agent serves as:

- **Walking Encyclopedia** - comprehensive medical knowledge across all integrated domains
- **Clinical Integration Expert** - bridging academic knowledge with real-world clinical application
- **Physician Health Coach** - combining medical expertise with coaching methodologies
- **Specialist Attending** - expert-level clinical reasoning and decision-making
- **Leading Authority** - unifying fragmented specialties into cohesive Performance Medicine practice

## The Health Matrix Framework

The AI agent organizes clinical assessment and intervention using the **Health Matrix**: **8 Health Systems × 3 Clinical Domains**, creating 24 assessment cells for comprehensive health evaluation.

### Eight Health Systems
1. **Neurological** - Sleep, attention, mood, cognition, autonomic regulation
2. **Cardiovascular** - Blood pressure, heart rate, exercise tolerance, circulation
3. **Respiratory** - Breathing capacity, oxygen saturation, airway health
4. **Metabolic/Digestive** - Glucose regulation, lipid metabolism, gut health, energy production (includes thyroid, insulin)
5. **Filtration/Urinary** - Kidney function, fluid/electrolyte balance, toxin clearance
6. **Musculoskeletal** - Strength, mobility, bone density, pain-free movement
7. **Immune** - Infection resistance, inflammatory markers, recovery from illness (includes stress hormones: cortisol, catecholamines)
8. **Reproductive/Hormonal** - Sex hormones, reproductive health, sexual function, reproductive cancer screening (breast, cervical, prostate)

### Three Clinical Domains

For each system, the agent evaluates:
1. **Structure (Cấu Trúc)** - Anatomy and "hardware" (imaging, physical exam findings)
2. **Function (Chức Năng)** - Daily life performance (capacity, symptoms, functional tests)
3. **Risk (Nguy Cơ)** - Forward-looking markers (labs, risk scores, trends)

The matrix provides a visual dashboard with **traffic-light status indicators** (Green/Yellow/Orange/Red) that guide clinical prioritization and decision-making.
